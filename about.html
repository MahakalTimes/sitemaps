About this Subdomain

sitemaps.mahakaltimes.com is a utility subdomain operated by MahakalTimes to host XML sitemaps and HTML link hubs. It helps search engines discover and index our content cleanly, while keeping Google Search Console (GSC) free of non-essential or third-party URLs.

What lives here

  • Sitemap Index (XML): points only to our domain sitemaps (e.g., mahakaltimes.com and /hi).
  • Backlink Hub (HTML): lists external mentions and references. These are not included in XML sitemaps.
  • Press Coverage / Tier-3 Hubs (HTML): optional catalogs of earned media and low-priority links.

  • External/third-party links belong in HTML pages (not in any XML sitemap).
  • We may revise file names and structures to improve crawl quality; see Changelog.

Data & Privacy

This subdomain is static and does not collect personal data. Any analytics or logs are limited to standard hosting/CDN telemetry. For details, see our Privacy Policy.
